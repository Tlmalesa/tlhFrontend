About Us

At Web Partner, we’re more than just your average team, we are a family of creatives dedicated to serving small businesses. We believe in championing values of exceptional service and quality products at affordable prices. Our passion and love for websites is fueled by the inspiring story each small business has to tell. With a decade of experience in web design and online marketing, we’ve had the honour of completing more than 10 000 projects and commit ourselves to helping our family of clients stay ahead of the game. We believe our success as a team comes from working together. If you’re looking to partner with a team of diverse and talented individuals in pursuit of breaking creative barriers, then we’d love to hear from you!
